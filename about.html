About BrandPull Services Pvt Ltd

BrandPull Services Pvt Ltd started as a freelancing venture in 2021, founded by DurgaPrasad K, with a vision to transform businesses through comprehensive digital solutions. Our journey began with a landmark achievement, serving OOTBox, where we successfully sold 8 franchises in just one year. We provided OOTBox with a full suite of services, including social media marketing (SMM), search engine optimization (SEO), lead generation, and deal closures.

After serving 10+ clients as a freelancer, BrandPull Services Pvt Ltd was officially registered as a company in 2024. Today, we are a thriving team located at Plot No. 62, 2nd Floor, Arunodaya Colony, Madhapur, Hyderabad - 500081, with virtual offices in Mumbai and Bangalore. Under the leadership of CEO and Creative Head DurgaPrasad K and Director Mr. Rajesh Pattani, our team of SEO specialists, social media marketers, designers, and developers work collaboratively to deliver exceptional results.

BrandPull Services is proudly recognized by StartUp India, reflecting our commitment to fostering innovation and supporting business growth nationwide and internationally. Our mission is to be the ultimate partner for startups, offering IT and non-IT services that drive success and expansion.
